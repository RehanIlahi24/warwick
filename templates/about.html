About Le CoQ

Welcome to Le CoQ – Your Culinary Haven at the University!

At Le CoQ, we believe in crafting more than just meals; we strive to create an experience that indulges your senses and elevates your dining moments. Nestled within the heart of the university, Le CoQ is more than a cafe and restaurant – it's a culinary journey that promises freshness, innovation, and a dash of delightful indulgence.

Our Philosophy

Freshness Redefined: Le CoQ takes pride in sourcing the finest and freshest ingredients. From locally-grown produce to carefully selected meats and seafood, we are committed to delivering a menu that not only tantalizes your taste buds but also supports local communities.

Innovative Flavors: Our chefs are culinary artists, passionate about infusing every dish with creativity and innovation. Explore a menu that seamlessly blends classic favorites with bold, inventive flavors, offering a diverse palette for every discerning diner.

Warm Ambiance: Immerse yourself in the cozy and welcoming ambiance of Le CoQ. Whether you're here for a quick coffee break, a leisurely lunch, or a celebratory dinner, our space is designed to enhance your dining experience.

Our Story

Founded on the principles of sustainability, quality, and community, Le CoQ has become a hub for students, faculty, and visitors alike. From hearty breakfasts to delectable dinners, we aim to be the go-to destination for those seeking not just a meal but an escape into the world of culinary delight.

Our Team

Meet the passionate individuals behind Le CoQ – a team dedicated to bringing you exceptional dining experiences. From our skilled chefs crafting culinary masterpieces to our friendly waitstaff ensuring seamless service, each member of our team plays a vital role in making your visit memorable.

Visit Us

Discover the unique charm of Le CoQ at Harwick University. Whether you're grabbing a coffee between classes, enjoying a leisurely lunch with friends, or savoring a gourmet dinner, Le CoQ welcomes you to savor the moments and indulge in the extraordinary. Join us at Le CoQ, where every dish tells a story, and every visit is an invitation to savor the extraordinary.
