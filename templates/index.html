Nourishing Minds for a Sustainable Tomorrow

At Warwick University, we take pride in our commitment to providing more than just meals; we deliver a sustainable dining experience that aligns with our values. Warwick Eats, the university's premier food service, operates across various locations on campus, ensuring that every bite contributes to a sustainable future.

  • Local Sourcing: We've been dedicated to supporting local agriculture. Warwick Eats has a defined purchasing target to source seasonal ingredients from nearby food growers, ensuring freshness and reducing our carbon footprint.

  • Animal Welfare: Warwick Eats has been a pioneer in reducing antibiotic use in farm animals. We believe in responsible farming practices that prioritize the well-being of our animals.

  • Quality Dairy: We've committed to serving rBGH-free milk, contributing to a healthier and more sustainable dairy industry.

  • Egg-cellent Choices: Warwick Eats adopted cage-free eggs, promoting humane practices in egg production.

  • Sustainable Seafood: We've strived to serve seafood that meets the stringent guidelines of Seafood Watch® for commercial buyers. This commitment ensures that our seafood choices contribute to the health of our oceans.
